Selection of material:

We did, do and probably always will use, translate, share and promote:

  • Everything we feel good, healthy and interesting, including latest RFS, W3G proposals, main and supplemental specs, event reports and so.

  • Material not have to, but heavily better if, is part of PUBLIC DOMAIN, GNU or similar licensing.

  • Actual materials will have higher priority. Actuality is some product of corporate, community and volunteers support, plus some maturity coefficient

Two big NO`s and three smaller no`s. Some kind of materials we do not and probably never will take care of:

  • Papers, which is strictly classified, limited in use, taken from unknown sources etc. i.e. WE DO NOT USE LEAKS

  • Papers, generated from parties, known as persistent source of trials and prosecution. We have no man power to clear them out

  • Outdated, obsolete, historical and limited value papers have some chance some day, which rarely happens

  • Some directions are good and interesting, but we can not obligate
